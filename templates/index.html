Welcome to Song Sonar!

New releases of artists you follow

Never miss a release again. Every friday, Song Sonar gathers all new releases of artists you follow in a spotify playlist, just for you.

Connect with spotify

Play all the releases

There is no song limit - your Song Sonar playlist will contain every release by every artist you follow.

Fresh tunes every friday

Song Sonar is updated every friday, just like Release Radar. Songs from the previous week will be removed from your playlist, so be sure to save the bangers!

Free as in freedom

Song Sonar is open source software. You can install it on your own machine, hack the code or just use the free service hosted on songsonar.rocks.
